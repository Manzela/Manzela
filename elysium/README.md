# Elysium — Physical-Context AI for Retail Intelligence

An end-to-end AI product case study covering the full lifecycle: Discovery → Architecture → Implementation → Strategic Decision.

## Overview

Elysium is a multi-agent Generative AI platform built on Google Vertex AI, designed to bridge the gap between physical retail locations and hyper-local, real-time demand data. This portfolio documents the complete product journey — from enterprise stakeholder interviews to patent-pending innovations to a data-driven strategic pause.

## Highlights

- **13 enterprise brands validated** across 4 continents (IL, US, PT, ES, MX) — drawn from a broader 20+ stakeholder interview pool
- **11-agent architecture** on Vertex AI Agent Builder with Bison-002 production + Gemini 1.0 Pro evaluated
- **15 patent claims** (3 independent + 12 dependent) including H3 geospatial indexing and grammar-constrained decoding
- **15,600+ store locations** in scope (5,000+ grocery · 600+ wholesale · 10,000+ pharmacy)
- **Privacy-first data architecture** with GDPR compliance and 3-tier access model
- **3 published research articles** on AI personalization and retail economics
