What it proved · Four claims

The pattern across nine years

  1. Pipeline-thinking before agent-thinking.

    Frame any system as a chain of deterministic filters before adding probabilistic compute.

    Source: Data Mining (2019).

  2. Agentic patterns ship without the term.

    The four-layer Classify / Retrieve / Execute / Verify pattern was load-bearing in 2020.

    Source: Tasko AI (2020 — 23).

  3. Discovery-validated PMF can lose to sales-cycle structure.

    The right product can be the wrong order of operations.

    Source: Elysium (2024 — 25).

  4. Fail-closed is the only viable production posture for autonomous content at scale.

    The gate fires first; the agent activates only if the gate passes. 80% deterministic / 20% probabilistic compute.

    Source: Pipeline (2024 — present).
