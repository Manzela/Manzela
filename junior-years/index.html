00.2Gigs

The ledger.

  1. 01
    Personal Assistance 2017 — 2019 · seasonal

    Working as a personal driver and household helper for wealthy families. Alongside this, picked up whatever the families and their networks needed done digitally: web development (WordPress, HTML/CSS/JS — landing pages, marketing sites, webfronts), Shopify & WooCommerce store setups, inventory imports from ERP to web (manual extract, transform, load), market research and competitor briefs, spreadsheet automation (VBA, formula chains, dashboards), and business-plan support for founders fundraising or applying to grants.

  2. 02
    BoldKing and Rimoni Plast 2016 — 2017

    Automated Manufacturing Line machine operator: operating a large manufacturing line, troubleshooting physical machine issues and ensuring its working.

  3. 03
    Construction Labor 2015 — 2016

    Construction gigs, painting houses and site cleanup.

  4. 04
    Service & waitressing 2014 — 2017

    Working as a waiter on weekends and covering shifts as a security guard in outdoor events.

  5. 05
    Warehouse 2013 — 2014

    Stocking goods and inventory, and moving heavy inventory for sports brands like Mizuno and Wertheimer Sports.

00.3Looking back

Manually moving inventory between an ERP export and a Shopify import is, structurally, the same work as a five-stage scraping pipeline. The format changes; the operation — extract, normalize, enrich, format, deliver — does not. Three years of doing it by hand built the reflex before there was a pipeline to put it in.
